Product Overview

Rasad (also known as Metro) v1.0 is a comprehensive railway/metro safety monitoring system designed to enhance passenger safety on metro platforms. The system is designed to detect train entry and exit at metro stations, as well as monitor passenger crossing of the yellow safety line at platform edges to enhance operational and passenger safety. The system automatically detects train movement and passenger safety violations, providing real-time alerts to station operators for immediate response and enhanced safety management.

Technical Specifications

The Rasad Metro system employs a dual-sensor architecture optimized for metro safety monitoring. Tunnel-installed sensors dedicated to train arrival and departure detection support a maximum sensing distance of up to 12 meters, enabling reliable detection of train movement in tunnel environments. Platform edge sensors installed along the yellow safety line provide passenger safety monitoring with a maximum detection range of up to 200 meters, ensuring comprehensive coverage of platform areas for passenger safety violation detection.

Communication and Configuration: Sensor communication is based on the NRF wireless protocol operating at 2.4 GHz frequency, ensuring reliable data transmission in metro station environments. Each sensor operates at 12V with 1A current consumption, providing efficient power utilization for continuous monitoring operations. Sensors include an internal wireless interface for configuration and parameter tuning, enabling flexible setup and adjustment of detection parameters without physical access to sensor units.

System Integration: The system is fully compatible with Sedna Metro Manager v1.0 software, providing centralized monitoring, configuration, and safety management capabilities. This integration enables comprehensive metro safety system management, allowing operators to monitor multiple detection points across stations, configure system parameters, and manage safety alerts from a unified platform.

Purpose and Use Cases

The system addresses critical safety concerns in metro operations by monitoring train entry and exit events, detecting passenger safety violations such as crossing the yellow safety line, identifying unauthorized access to restricted areas, and providing immediate visual and audio warnings to station staff. The system's emphasis on passenger safety, reliable train detection, and real-time monitoring capabilities makes it essential for maintaining safe metro operations and protecting passengers in metro environments.

Key Applications

  • Metro platform safety monitoring
  • Train movement tracking in tunnels
  • Passenger safety line violation detection
  • Tunnel intrusion detection
  • Platform door status monitoring
  • Real-time train arrival and departure detection
  • Passenger safety alert systems

Target Users

Metro station operators, railway safety personnel, transit authority managers, and public transportation safety coordinators responsible for passenger safety in metro systems.
