DepoGraph V2.5

Enhanced 3D Scanning System

← Back to Projects

Product Overview

DepoGraph V2.5 represents the most advanced evolution of our 3D scanning technology, featuring enhanced performance, improved accuracy, and expanded operational capabilities for the most demanding professional measurement applications. This professional-grade solution offers the highest level of functionality, supporting both fixed and portable operation modes to accommodate diverse deployment requirements.

Technical Specifications

The DepoGraph V2.5 delivers exceptional scanning capability up to 210 meters with the widest field of view in the series: 270° horizontal and 70° vertical coverage. Operating at 12V with 6A current consumption, the system provides robust power handling for extended operation. Data transmission is supported via both LAN and wireless connectivity, offering maximum flexibility for various deployment scenarios. The system is compatible with Sedna Studio v1.0, v2.0, and V Fix software platforms, providing comprehensive software ecosystem support.

Advanced Features: Unlike previous versions, the V2.5 supports auto-merge functionality, enabling seamless integration of multiple scan sessions. The system includes IMU and GPS synchronization capabilities for precise georeferencing and orientation tracking. Additionally, the V2.5 is equipped with an integrated camera for visual documentation and includes a laser pointer for precise targeting and alignment during setup.

Environmental Protection: Designed for outdoor environments with IP67 protection rating, the system is fully protected against dust ingress and water immersion, making it suitable for operation in harsh industrial and field conditions.

Purpose and Use Cases

This professional-grade version provides superior scanning performance for applications requiring the highest levels of precision, reliability, and operational flexibility in 3D spatial measurement and documentation. The combination of extended range, wide field of view, environmental protection, and advanced features makes it the ideal solution for critical measurement applications.

Key Applications

  • High-precision industrial measurement
  • Quality assurance and inspection
  • Detailed spatial documentation
  • Advanced volume analysis
  • Professional surveying applications
  • Large-scale outdoor mapping projects
  • Fixed installation monitoring systems

Target Users

Professional surveyors, quality assurance engineers, and technical specialists requiring the highest precision in 3D measurement applications.
