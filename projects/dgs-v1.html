Product Overview

The DGS (Aircraft Docking Guidance System) is a mission-critical, airport-grade automated guidance system designed to safely guide pilots to the correct stop position during aircraft parking operations at airport gates. The system uses advanced laser scanning technology combined with computer vision to detect aircraft position in real-time and provide accurate guidance information. Pilot guidance is delivered via a dedicated visual display, providing clear and intuitive alignment and distance information to ensure precise aircraft positioning and enhanced operational safety.

Technical Specifications

The DGS system features a maximum aircraft detection range of up to 210 meters, enabling early detection and tracking of approaching aircraft for comprehensive guidance throughout the docking process. The system is equipped with dual wide-angle 5-megapixel cameras for accurate aircraft recognition and positioning, ensuring reliable identification and tracking across various aircraft types and sizes.

Power Requirements: The display unit operates at 220V with 10A current consumption, while the core system operates at 220V with 5A current consumption, providing robust power handling for continuous operation in demanding airport environments.

Connectivity and Integration: The system supports wireless networking for technical configuration and system setup, enabling flexible deployment and maintenance access. Additionally, the system includes LAN connectivity for stable data communication and seamless integration with airport infrastructure systems. The DGS is fully compatible with Sedna Airport Manager software, providing centralized monitoring, configuration, and comprehensive system management capabilities for airport operations.

Environmental Reliability: Designed for reliable operation in various weather and environmental conditions, the system features temperature-controlled hardware to ensure consistent performance and accuracy across temperature extremes, making it suitable for deployment in diverse climate zones and challenging airport environments.

Purpose and Use Cases

As a mission-critical airport-grade solution, the DGS system ensures accurate positioning of aircraft at gate parking positions, enhances safety by providing precise distance and direction information to pilots, improves operational efficiency by reducing docking time, and enables automated recording of docking operations. The system's focus on safety, reliability, and operational efficiency makes it an essential component of modern airport ground operations infrastructure.

Key Applications

  • Aircraft gate docking operations
  • Airport ground operations
  • Aircraft positioning and alignment
  • Ground marshal assistance
  • Docking operation documentation
  • Pilot guidance and safety enhancement
  • Airport infrastructure integration

Target Users

Airport ground operations staff, aircraft marshals, airline ground crews, and airport management personnel responsible for safe and efficient aircraft docking operations.
