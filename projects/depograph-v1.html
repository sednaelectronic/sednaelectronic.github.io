Product Overview

DepoGraph V1.0 is a precision 3D scanning system designed for creating detailed point cloud representations of indoor environments. The system employs a two-axis mechanical gimbal mechanism with a laser distance sensor to systematically scan and map spatial data.

Purpose and Use Cases

The system is designed for inventory management, volume calculation, space planning, documentation, and quality control applications. It enables accurate 3D mapping of storage facilities, warehouses, and other indoor spaces.

Key Applications

  • 3D mapping of storage facilities and warehouses
  • Accurate volume measurements of storage areas
  • Creating 3D models for layout optimization
  • Generating point cloud records of spaces
  • Verifying spatial dimensions and layouts

Target Users

Warehouse managers, logistics professionals, facility planners, and quality control specialists who need accurate spatial data for operational planning and documentation.
